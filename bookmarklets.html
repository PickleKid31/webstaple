Installation Instructions

  1. Find the section of the bookmarklet you wish to install, and click the link.
  2. Copy the code that you see.
  3. Right click your bookmarks bar.
  4. Click "add page."
  5. A window should pop up.
  6. In the box that says "url", put your copied code.
  7. In the box that says "name" put what you want your bookmarklet to be called.
  8. Click save, and you're done! Your bookmarklet should show up in your bookmarks bar!
